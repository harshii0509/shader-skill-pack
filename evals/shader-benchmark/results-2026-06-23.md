## Method

Structural source-grounding eval. `scripts/evaluate-benchmark.py` runs TF-IDF routing over the on-disk skill corpora (SKILL.md + references/*.md for each of the 6 skills) and checks four dimensions per case:

1. **Route** — TF-IDF predicted lane matches `expected_primary_lane`
2. **Reference coverage** — `expected_first_references` paths exist on disk
3. **Concept coverage** — `must_mention` terms are present in the expected lane's corpus
4. **Separation** — `must_avoid` terms are absent from the expected lane's corpus

No live model inference. This proves packaged source quality and routing discrimination, not live behavioral parity.
